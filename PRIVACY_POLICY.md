# Privacy Policy for Dayring

**Effective date:** 4 July 2026
**Last updated:** 4 July 2026

Dayring ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal day-planning app developed and
published by **Suhail Mullapullikattil** (an individual developer). This policy explains what
information the app handles and how. Contact: **mksuhail05@gmail.com**.

## Summary

Dayring is designed to be private by default. Everything you create — tasks,
checklists, habits, goals, workouts, notes and settings — is stored **only on
your device**. We do not run servers, we do not have accounts, and we do not
collect, sell, or transmit your personal planning data. The only data that
leaves your device is what the Google AdMob advertising service needs to show
ads (see "Advertising" below), and only if ads are enabled in your version of
the app.

## Information stored on your device

The following is created by you and saved locally on your device (using
on-device storage). It is never uploaded to us:

- Tasks, subtasks/checklists, reminders, and completion history
- Habits, goals, day templates, and streaks
- Workouts and exercise logs
- Daily journal notes
- Your schedule preferences (wake/sleep times, nudge settings)
- An optional app passcode (PIN), stored locally only

If you delete the app, this data is removed from your device. You can also
create a manual JSON backup file from Settings; that file is written to your
device's app storage and is under your control.

## Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you choose to connect Apple Health, Dayring reads the following **only with
your explicit permission**: step count, sleep, and workout summaries. This data
is read on your device to display your wellness information inside the app.

- Health data is **read-only** — Dayring does not write to Apple Health.
- Health data is **never transmitted off your device**, never shared with third
  parties, and **never used for advertising or marketing**.
- You can revoke access at any time in the iOS Settings app under Privacy &
  Security → Health.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

Dayring may display banner ads served by **Google AdMob**. To serve and measure
ads, Google may collect and process certain information such as a device
advertising identifier, IP address, and general ad-interaction data. This
processing is carried out by Google, not by us. For details, see:

- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

On iOS, Dayring will ask for your permission via Apple's App Tracking
Transparency prompt before any tracking-based (personalized) advertising is
used. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be
non-personalized. Purchasing the app's Pro upgrade removes ads.

**We never share Apple Health data with AdMob or any advertiser.**

## Notifications

Dayring uses **local notifications** (task reminders and optional daily
planning nudges) that are scheduled on your device. They are not sent through
any server and involve no collection of your data.

## What we do NOT do

- We do **not** require an account or sign-in.
- We do **not** use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
- We do **not** operate a backend server or cloud database.
- We do **not** sell your personal information.

## Children's privacy

Dayring is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. Advertising, where shown, is provided by
Google AdMob subject to its own policies.

## Data retention and deletion

Because your planning data lives only on your device, you control it entirely.
To delete all of it, uninstall the app. Advertising data handled by Google is
retained and deleted according to Google's own policies, linked above.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU GDPR or California CCPA),
you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we do not
hold your planning data on any server, you can exercise most of these rights
directly on your device. For questions about advertising data, please refer to
Google's policy, or contact us and we will help where we can.

## International users

The app runs on your device wherever you are. Any ad-related processing by
Google may involve transfer and processing in other countries under Google's
policies.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if features that
handle data are added or removed). Material changes will be reflected by an
updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you
accept the revised policy.
